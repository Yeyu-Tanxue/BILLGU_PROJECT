What it demonstrates

Motor-control thinking from circuit to firmware.

这个项目展示了从电路资料、PCB、驱动层代码到控制逻辑的完整嵌入式实践链路。公开仓库中保留了 可审阅的文档和源码，去掉了 IDE 缓存、编译输出和原始压缩包。

The project is useful as evidence of embedded C development, peripheral integration, motor feedback handling, and hardware-oriented debugging.
