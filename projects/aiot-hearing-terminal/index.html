AIoT Accessibility / 云边协同

听障双向沟通终端

ESP32-S3-CAM captures sign-language gestures, PC/cloud models recognize and polish the text, and the terminal supports LCD captions, ASR, TTS, and emergency interaction.

97-99%Recorded recognition rate in project notes
ESP32-S3Camera, LCD, microphone, speaker firmware
LSTM + CNNSign sequence and on-device gesture models

Pipeline

From gesture capture to natural sentence output.

边端负责采集和交互，PC/云端负责更重的识别与润色：ESP32 拍摄连续帧，经串口传输到 PC， MediaPipe 抽取关键点，LSTM 推理词语，再由大模型把词语序列润色成自然中文。

The same terminal also supports speech-to-text captions and text-to-speech playback, making it a two-way communication prototype rather than a single recognition demo.
